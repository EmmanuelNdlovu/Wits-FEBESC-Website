Who We Are

The Wits Faculty of Engineering and the Built Environment Students' Council (FEBESC) is the official student representative body serving all seven schools within the Faculty. Our mission is to foster student development, advocate for student interests, strengthen industry engagement, and create opportunities that empower every engineering and built environment student to thrive academically, professionally, and socially.
