EBE Council Energy Indaba 2025

About the Event

This energy indaba brought together students, leaders, professors, and industry experts from across the Faculty of Engineering and the Built Environment to discuss and explore sustainable energy solutions and innovations. In addition, the event featured keynote speeches, panel discussions, and workshops on renewable energy technologies and policies.

Event Highlights

  • Keynote speeches from the Minister of Electricity and Energy (Hon. Dr. K. Ramokgopa) and industry experts
  • Panel discussions on sustainable energy policies
  • Workshops on renewable energy technologies
  • Networking opportunities with faculty and industry leaders
