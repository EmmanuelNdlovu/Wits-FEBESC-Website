About the Council

The Faculty of Engineering and the Built Environment (FEBE) Students' Council at Wits University is an elected body that represents and advocates for the interests of all students in the faculty. Our aim is to foster academic excellence, inclusivity, and student engagement across all disciplines.

Our Schools

Our Responsibilities

  • Representing student interests in faculty boards and university structures
  • Organizing events, workshops, and initiatives to enhance student life
  • Disseminating important academic and funding information
  • Promoting unity and collaboration across engineering and built environment disciplines
